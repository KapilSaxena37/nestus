Frequently asked questions
How much does a hostel or PG cost?

Student hostels and PGs typically range from ₹3,500 to ₹15,000 per month depending on the city, sharing type (single/double/triple) and whether food and AC are included. Each listing shows its room types and prices clearly.

Are the listings verified?

Listings with the ✓ Verified badge have been checked by the NestUs team. Others are shown as unverified until we confirm them. Always look for the badge and photos.

How do I contact a hostel owner?

Open any listing and use "Contact owner" to call or WhatsApp them, or "Message owner" to chat inside NestUs. You can also schedule a visit.

Is there any brokerage or fee for students?

No. NestUs connects you directly with owners — there's no broker and no fee to search or contact listings.

Which cities does NestUs cover?

We're live in Nagpur, Indore, Pune, Bhopal and Kota, and adding more. Pick your city on the home page to browse.
